Meet our Farmers

In addition to helping the roosters, we work directly with our farmers to improve their quality of life. We send Naomi, Fernando's daughter to the trilingual school. Welcome to the Coffee Rooster

We care about coffee and we care about roosters. It's what we do, all of our sales go directly to helping roosters in need. We feel very passionately about helping our fowl friends. They deserve our compassion, and more importantly our respect.


We roast all of our own coffee beans in house on our farm property, we have a viewing window for the roosters to watch the roasters at work. They love each other!
